# NDA Template Source Documentation

## Template Origin and Purpose

This template represents a standard mutual non-disclosure agreement structure commonly used in business-to-business relationships. The template incorporates widely accepted legal provisions and industry best practices for confidentiality protection.

## Key Design Principles

### Comprehensive Coverage
- Includes all essential NDA clauses required for enforceability
- Balances protection of confidential information with practical business needs
- Provides standard legal exceptions to avoid overreach

### Clarity and Enforceability
- Uses plain language where possible while maintaining legal precision
- Includes specific definitions and obligations to reduce ambiguity
- Structured with clear sections and numbering for easy reference

### Flexibility
- Contains bracketed placeholders for customization to specific relationships
- Accommodates both mutual and unilateral confidentiality scenarios
- Allows for jurisdiction-specific modifications

## Template Sections Explained

1. **Parties and Date**: Establishes legal entities and effective date
2. **Purpose**: Defines the business reason for information sharing
3. **Confidential Information**: Broad but reasonable definition of protected information
4. **Obligations**: Specific duties of the receiving party
5. **Exceptions**: Standard legal limitations on confidentiality obligations
6. **Return of Materials**: Process for information return or destruction
7. **Term**: Duration of confidentiality obligations
8. **Remedies**: Enforcement mechanisms including equitable relief
9. **General Provisions**: Governing law, jurisdiction, and administrative clauses

## Usage Guidelines

This template serves as a baseline for comparison with AI-generated NDAs in the evaluation project. It represents professional legal drafting standards and includes provisions commonly required for enforceability in most jurisdictions.
