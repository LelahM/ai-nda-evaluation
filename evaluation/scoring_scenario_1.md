**Completeness (0/2)**

- **Missing high-risk clauses**: No injunctive relief provision, no governing law, no specific enforcement mechanisms

**Legal Structure (0/2)**
- Informal formatting without proper legal document structure
- No section numbering or professional organization
- Missing signature blocks with titles and dates

**Clarity & Precision (1/2)**
- Basic purpose stated but lacks specificity
- No defined timeframes except overall term
- Quote: "Information should be used only for the consulting engagement" - too general

**Risk Mitigation (0/2)**
- No injunctive relief clause
- Generic "legal action" reference without specifics
- Missing governing law and jurisdiction provisions
- **Critical gap**: No severability clause

**Business Alignment (1/2)**
- Recognizes mutual nature of relationship
- Consulting context acknowledged but not detailed
- Duration not aligned with stated 6-month engagement period

**Legal Structure (2/2)**

4. **Risk Management**: Injunctive relief and detailed obligations vs. minimal protection
5. **Business Fit**: Tailored to mutual consulting needs vs. generic confidentiality
