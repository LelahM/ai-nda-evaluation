- **Missing high-risk clauses**: No return/destruction provisions, no injunctive relief, no exceptions clause, no governing law
- Completely absent: severability, survival provisions, proper remedies

**Legal Structure (0/2)**
- Basic bullet-point format unsuitable for legal document
- No section organization or professional formatting
- Missing proper party identification and signature requirements

**Clarity & Precision (1/2)**
- Purpose somewhat clear but lacks detail
- 180-day term specified but no survival provisions
- Healthcare context mentioned but not properly addressed

**Risk Mitigation (0/2)**
- Generic "legal action" threat without specific remedies
- No consideration of healthcare data sensitivity
- **Critical healthcare gap**: No HIPAA or regulatory compliance considerations
- Missing jurisdiction and governing law provisions

**Business Alignment (1/2)**

- Unilateral nature partially addressed
- Duration reasonable for vendor evaluation but lacks survival provisions
