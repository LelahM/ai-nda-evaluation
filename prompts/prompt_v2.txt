Draft a comprehensive Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) for the following business scenario:

PURPOSE: Create a legally structured NDA that protects confidential information while enabling legitimate business discussions.

APPLICABLE LAW: Structure the agreement to be enforceable under common law principles with standard legal provisions.

REQUIRED SECTIONS:
1. Parties and effective date
2. Definition of confidential information (broad but reasonable scope)
3. Permitted and prohibited uses
4. Obligations of receiving party
5. Exceptions to confidentiality (standard legal exceptions)
6. Return or destruction of information
7. Term and termination
8. Remedies and enforcement (including injunctive relief)
9. General provisions (governing law, jurisdiction, severability)

CLARITY REQUIREMENTS:
- Use plain language where possible while maintaining legal precision
- Define technical or business-specific terms
- Structure with numbered sections and clear headings
- Include specific time periods and deadlines
- Address mutual vs. unilateral confidentiality as appropriate

Ensure the document balances protection of confidential information with practical business needs and enforceability.
