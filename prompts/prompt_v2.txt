Draft a mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) for the following business scenario.

REQUIREMENTS:

Governing Law: New York

Structure the agreement with numbered sections and clear headings covering:
1. Parties and effective date
2. Purpose of the agreement
3. Definition of confidential information (specific but reasonable scope)
4. Permitted and prohibited uses
5. Obligations of receiving party (use limitations and care standards)
6. Exceptions to confidentiality (standard legal exclusions: public information, prior knowledge, independent development, lawful disclosure requirements)
7. Return or destruction of materials
8. Term and survival of obligations
9. Remedies for breach (including injunctive relief where appropriate)
10. Governing law, jurisdiction, and general provisions (severability, entire agreement, amendments)

Format requirements:
- Use clear, plain language where possible
- Define technical or business-specific terms
- Include specific timeframes for key obligations
- Structure obligations so they can be evaluated against clear criteria

The draft should be suitable for rubric-based evaluation and attorney review.
